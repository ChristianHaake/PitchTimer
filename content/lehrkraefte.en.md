# For Teachers

PitchTimer is a tool developed specifically for the educational sector to practice presentations and short pitches under realistic time conditions.

## Classroom Scenarios

**1. Elevator Pitches & Startup Ideas**
Have students present their business ideas in exactly 60 or 90 seconds. The strict time limit encourages them to focus on the essentials.

**2. Book Reports (One-Minute Talks)**
Use the timer for very short presentations of books or films. Students learn to build suspense and formulate their most important arguments precisely.

**3. Exam Preparation**
Oral exams often have fixed time windows for free speaking. With PitchTimer, learners can independently train their sense of timing.

## How to use PitchTimer

* **Pitch Stations**: Set up stations with tablets or laptops in the classroom. Students can practice independently or give each other feedback.
* **Presentation Mode**: Use the "Fullscreen" button to display the timer largely on the smartboard or projector. All distracting elements are hidden.
* **Notes Field**: Learners can write down their bullet points directly in PitchTimer, format them, and save them as a file to continue working on them in the next lesson.

## Privacy in the Classroom

PitchTimer is **privacy-friendly by design**.
All entered notes and history data are stored exclusively **locally in the browser**. There is no login, no cloud storage, and no tracking by us. You can use the tool in the classroom without hesitation.
